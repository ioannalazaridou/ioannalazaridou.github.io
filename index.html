I'm Ioanna, a UX/UI designer and front-end developer based in London, UK, who loves creating fantastic user experiences through design. I hold a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction from UCL and have over three years of work experience in UX/UI design, primarily focused on automotive technology and digital solutions for vehicle inspection and appraisal. I've contributed to significant European projects in smart energy systems, and my experience spans both end-user and B2B applications. Additionally, I hold a BSc degree in Management Science and Technology, providing me with a strong foundation in business thinking and programming skills. This combination enables me to create products that meet both user needs and business goals.
Besides my love for user-centric design, I enjoy traveling, painting and playing tennis.

If you want to learn more about me, let’s have a talk, I’d love to hear from you!
